About Us

Harmony Healthcare Group is committed to delivering comprehensive and compassionate healthcare services. Our mission is to support individuals and families with accessible, respectful, and person-centred care.

What We Do

We provide a wide range of healthcare services to meet your needs, including:

General Health Checkups

Routine health assessments to keep your wellness on track and detect any issues early.

Podiatry Services

Expert foot care through our partnership with Sydney Foot Doctor for mobility and comfort.

Allied Health Referrals

Connections to physiotherapists, dietitians, psychologists, and more for holistic care.

NDIS Participant Support

Specialised services tailored to support independence and quality of life for NDIS participants.

In-Home Visits

Convenient mobile care brought right to your doorstep for easier access to services.

Rehabilitation Support

Programs to help clients recover and regain functionality after illness, injury or surgery.

Our Support

Our experienced team works with families, GPs, and care coordinators to develop tailored plans. We believe in care that is:

  • Respectful – We treat all clients with dignity and value their input.
  • Reliable – Our team shows up and follows through, every time.
  • Responsive – Fast, clear communication with real results.
  • Flexible – Services built around your schedule and your needs.
